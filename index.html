About me

Oceans Landing was founded by Peter Dean Noel in 2017. Peter is a young professional Web Developer, a New York native born in Montreal, Canada and is currently residing in Brooklyn, NY. In 2009, Peter obtained his degree in Graphic Arts and Design from Touro College. Peter was born a visionary; as a visionary, he has always had a unique eye for art and utilized design to express his perceptions. Peter enjoys shooting a variety of types of photography including landscape, portraits, boudoir and many other forms. His passion for design and photography has allowed him to pursue his dreams while creating a successful business. Peter is an independent freelance Web Developer with an open mind and eye to all forms of design, for prices and consulting, please inquire.
